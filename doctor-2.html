Biography

Dr. Dasari is a board certified pediatrician and a fellow of American Academy of Pediatrics since 2012. He was trained in Lincoln Medical Center an affiliate of Weil Cornell University after his medical education from Gandhi Medical College,India. Dr. Dasari believes that children are the future of a nation and every child deserves a high quality pediatric care. He is well known for his amazing bedside manners and clinical acumen. His special interests include ADHD and allergies.

After his residency Dr. Dasari moved to Texas to work as an Assistant Professor @ Texas Tech University and started his own busy practice shortly thereafter. Patient care and satisfaction has always been his top priority. He believes that every child is unique and he loves to relate to them in that special way that they understand.

Dr.Dasari is a big Dallas cowboys fan and enjoys other outdoor sports like – Volleyball, tennis and NFL but his personal favorite is Cricket…in the 3rd paragraph

When not working she enjoys spending time reading, travelling and cooking. She is married to a pediatrician and has two boys ( 11 and 8 ). Having kids at work and home she says is the best part of her life and she loves to learn from them every day.

Dr .Dasari received best teaching award in Pediatrics for 2014-2015.
