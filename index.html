Welcome !

Welcome to Lone Star Pediatrics where your child's health is our first priority. Our staff and providers take pride in going above and beyond in helping our patients at any given day. We provide same day sick appointments, whether you have one child or 4 children the quality remains the same and we are happy to work with your requirements and needs. Our mission is to help with physical, mental and emotional development of your child and aim to be a "patient centered medical home". We have an after hours text line where parents can get guidance on any urgent medical needs. Please take a moment to visit our website and get to know us better and thank you for trusting the care of your child with Lone Star Pediatrics! 

Blog Articles
